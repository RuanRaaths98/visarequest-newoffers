Questions

Short Stay - FAQ

Typical requirements include a valid passport, passport-sized photos, completed application forms, proof of accommodation, return flight bookings, bank statements (last 3 months), travel insurance, and an invitation letter if applicable. We provide a detailed checklist specific to your destination country after your enquiry.
We recommend applying at least 6–8 weeks before your intended travel date for Schengen and European visas. Some embassies accept applications up to 6 months in advance. For tight timelines, ask about our Emergency Service.
Courier delivery to the embassy is included in the Premium package. Standard package clients submit their own documents or use our Standard package guidance to prepare their own visit to the visa centre.

What Counts as an Emergency?

Situations We Help With

Embassies recognise specific circumstances as qualifying emergencies. We assess your situation quickly and guide you on the best course of action - even before you've committed to the service.

  • 🏥
    Medical Emergency Abroad A family member is critically ill or hospitalised in another country.
  • 🕊️
    Bereavement A close family member has passed away and you need to travel immediately.
  • 💼
    Critical Business Travel An urgent, time-sensitive business obligation requires immediate travel.
  • 📋
    Humanitarian or Legal Need Legal proceedings, court appearances, or humanitarian situations requiring urgent travel.
  • ⏰
    Upcoming Travel Window Your planned travel is imminent and your standard application hasn't been processed in time.

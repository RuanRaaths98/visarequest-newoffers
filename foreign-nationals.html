What We Help With

End-to-End Business Support

From your first company registration to ongoing compliance, we provide or coordinate every service a foreign national needs to operate legally in South Africa.

Service 1

Business Setup & Company Registration

A complete "start-to-operate" package for foreign nationals establishing a presence in South Africa.

  • Company registration with CIPC
  • Name reservation
  • MOI (Memorandum of Incorporation) drafting
  • Director appointments & changes
  • Beneficial ownership compliance
  • BEE Certification
Service 2

SARS & Tax Registration

Get registered with the South African Revenue Service quickly and correctly - one of the most critical steps for any business operating in South Africa.

  • Tax number registration with SARS
  • VAT registration
  • PAYE, UIF & SDL registration
  • Import & export code registration
  • Tax clearance certificates (TCS)
Service 3

Accounting, Payroll & Compliance

Ongoing monthly services to keep your business financially compliant and your team paid correctly.

  • Monthly bookkeeping
  • Financial statements (annual)
  • Payroll processing (PAYE, UIF, SDL submissions)
  • EMP201 & EMP501 submissions
  • VAT returns
  • Management accounts
Service 4

Banking & Financial Setup

Helping you become financially operational as quickly as possible - from opening your first business account to setting up international payments.

  • Business bank account setup (Standard Bank, FNB, etc.)
  • Assistance with FICA compliance
  • Introductions to banking partners
  • Forex & international payment setup guidance
Service 5

Legal & Compliance Services

Protect your business and directors with the right legal structure from the start.

  • Shareholder agreements
  • Director resolutions
  • Employment contracts
  • BEE advisory
  • POPIA compliance (data protection)
  • Contract drafting
Service 6

Business Licensing & Industry Compliance

Sector-specific registrations and licensing requirements depending on the nature of your business.

  • Trading licenses (municipal)
  • Health & safety compliance
  • Sector-specific registrations (construction, transport, etc.)
  • COIDA registration
